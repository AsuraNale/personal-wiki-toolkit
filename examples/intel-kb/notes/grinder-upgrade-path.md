# Grinder upgrade path for espresso

The grinder matters more than the machine for espresso quality — a curated summary
of the upgrade path the owner is weighing, kept as Gold (promoted from the brief on
2026-05-20).

## The tiers (as they keep coming up in sources)

- **Entry conical (stepped)** — usable espresso, but stepped adjustment fights
  fine dial-in; alignment is often the limiting factor, not the burr.
  (source: home-barista thread, 2026-05-12)
- **Single-dose flat burr** — cleaner cup separation, low retention; the current
  consensus "first serious upgrade." Downside: price and workflow (weigh in / weigh
  out). (source: coffee-blog review, 2026-05-18)
- **High-end flat / prosumer** — diminishing returns for a home hobbyist unless
  chasing light-roast clarity. Flagged as "probably past the point that matters for
  the owner's medium-roast habit."
